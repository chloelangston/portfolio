Freewriting exercise: Pour all your fears into the abyss

Unload your fears into this blank empty abyss in the form of a text box. On submit, its contents, similar to my career, will go nowhere. Answer questions like why don't you enjoy public speaking? Better yet, why do you hate it? Why do you dread it? Write it all down. Include down your past experiences, particularly the terrible. Don't think about grammar or style. Just write.
